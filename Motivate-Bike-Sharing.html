Motivate US Bike Share

Motivate is bike sharing company that operates in major cities of CA, OR,W ashington DC, TN, NY, OH, I L and MA, under different brand names. Motivate makes their ride data public for anyone to analyze.

The users of Motivate are either a Subscriber who pays a monthly fee, and a reduced-rate ride fee, or a Customer who just shares the bike on demand and spot-payment basis. Customer dont pay any monthly fee, so their ride is is slightly higher than the Customers.

In this project we analyzied the ride data for 2016, for 3 major cities: NY City, Chicago, and Washington DC.

The project files are available in in github.
